About Us

Mr Ravi Damani
 

 Ravishal Silver House has a rich 35+ years experience, and is one of the most renowned Silver Smiths in India. The Company embarked on this journey in 1985 by starting the retail sale of Silver products in India. It quickly backward integrated into manufacturing as the Company was unable source high quality products to cater to its HNI clientele. As India started on its journey of liberalisation in 1991, Indian consumer started experiencing world-class products of luxury. But, the Indian silver industry was unable to satisfy the high quality appetite of this micro-market.
  • In the year 2000, Mr. Ravi Damani travelled to U.K to develop International clientele for the Company.
  • He tied up with several retailers in London (The Silver Vault). His unique product design, make-to-order approach, and ability to create intricate design got the Company many accolades.
  • Many of these customers have stood with the Company for over 2 decades.
  • As the company embraces the e-commerce platform, Company has now embarked on a direct sale to consumer model.
    • Customers can now choose from our extensive catagloue to give us a unique make-to-order product.
  • The Company is very young, and is confident that our best work is ahead of us.
